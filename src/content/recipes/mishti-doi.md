## Ingredients

- Whole milk
- Sugar (caramelized)
- Yogurt culture (a spoonful of existing yogurt)

## Steps

1. Reduce milk slowly until thickened.
2. Caramelize sugar and stir into the milk.
3. Cool to lukewarm, add yogurt culture.
4. Set overnight in clay pots or a warm spot.
