## Ingredients

- Flattened rice (chira/poha)
- Mustard seeds
- Onion, fresh coconut, peanuts

## Steps

1. Rinse and soften the flattened rice.
2. Temper mustard seeds and onion in oil.
3. Add coconut and peanuts, then fold in the rice.
4. Season and serve warm.
