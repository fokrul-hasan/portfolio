## Ingredients

- Basmati rice
- Mutton
- Yogurt, ginger, garlic paste
- Potatoes
- Saffron, ghee

## Steps

1. Marinate the mutton overnight.
2. Par-cook the rice.
3. Layer mutton, potatoes, and rice in a heavy pot.
4. Seal and slow-cook ("dum") until fully done.
