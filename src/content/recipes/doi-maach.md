## Ingredients

- Rohu fish
- Yogurt
- Mustard paste
- Green chilies, onion, turmeric

## Steps

1. Lightly fry the fish pieces.
2. Make a base of onion, turmeric, and mustard paste.
3. Add whisked yogurt and simmer gently (don't boil hard, or it splits).
4. Add fish back in and finish with green chilies.
