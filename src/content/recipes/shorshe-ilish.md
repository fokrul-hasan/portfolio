## Ingredients

- Hilsa (ilish) fish
- Mustard seed paste
- Green chilies
- Mustard oil, turmeric

## Steps

1. Coat fish pieces with mustard paste, turmeric, and salt.
2. Add mustard oil and slit green chilies.
3. Steam or gently simmer until the fish is cooked through.
