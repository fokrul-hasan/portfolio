## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Ghee
- Sugar
- Cardamom, milk powder

## Steps

1. Whisk eggs thoroughly.
2. Cook in ghee over low heat, stirring constantly.
3. Add sugar and milk powder, keep stirring until it comes together.
4. Finish with crushed cardamom.
